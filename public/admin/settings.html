Table Flyer

Choose how each table's QR flyer looks. Default uses our branded artwork with your restaurant's name and a QR code. Custom lets you upload your own design — we'll place just the QR code centered on top of it.

After uploading, use "Regenerate Flyer" on each table (in Tables & QR) to apply it.

Cloudinary Account

Your menu photos, logo, and QR flyers are hosted on your own Cloudinary account. Your platform administrator usually sets this up when your restaurant is created — use this only to check what's connected or to switch to a different Cloudinary account.
